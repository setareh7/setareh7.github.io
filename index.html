About Me

I am a PhD student and a member of the Natural Language Dialogue research group at USC Institute for Creative Technologies, working under the supervision of Prof. David Traum. My research focus is multimodal feedback generation in dialogue, multimodal human-computer interaction, and natural language dialogue systems.
