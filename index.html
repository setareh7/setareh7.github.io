About Me

I got my PhD degree in Computer Scienc from University of Southern California. I was a member of the Natural Language Dialogue research group at USC Institute for Creative Technologies, working under the supervision of Prof. David Traum. My research focus was multimodal feedback understanding and generation in human-agent interactions, multimodal human-computer interaction, and natural language dialogue systems.
